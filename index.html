About Me

As an Assistant Lecturer at Werabe University since June 2021, I am driven by a passion for education and a commitment to nurturing the minds of tomorrow's leaders. With a background in Health Informatics, I bring a wealth of knowledge and real-world experience to the classroom. My dedication to fostering a dynamic learning environment empowers students to explore, question, and innovate, preparing them for success in their academic and professional endeavors.


In addition to teaching, I am actively involved in Health Management Information system (HMIS), where I strive to contribute to the academic community through research, mentorship, and collaboration. My goal is to inspire a love for learning, instill critical thinking skills, and empower students to make meaningful contributions to society.

Join me on this journey of discovery and transformation as we embark on a quest for knowledge and excellence together.
